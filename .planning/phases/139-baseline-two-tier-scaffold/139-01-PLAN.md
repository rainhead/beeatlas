<objective>
Establish the v4.8 test-suite measurement anchor and the two-tier marker harness for the `data/` pytest suite.

Two independent deliverables:
1. **BASELINE.md** (TPERF-01) — a committed living doc recording the current suite runtime as a per-tier ESTIMATE (NOT a full timed run), the two targets, the dominant cost contributors, and the honest ~19-failure red inventory.

The marker decision criterion is "does this test validate code or validate data?", not "is it slow."
</objective>
