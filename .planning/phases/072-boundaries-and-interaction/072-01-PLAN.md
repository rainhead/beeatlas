# Plan 072-01: Boundary layers and click interaction (sketch)

Detailed planning deferred until Phase 71 is complete. High-level scope:

1. **Boundary layers**: Add county and ecoregion GeoJSON as Mapbox sources + fill/line layers. Toggle visibility via `boundaryMode`. Highlight selected boundaries via `feature-state`.

2. **Click handlers**: Use `map.queryRenderedFeatures(point, { layers })` for hit detection.
   - Click on unclustered point → `map-click-occurrence` (single)
   - Click on cluster → `map-click-occurrence` (multi) with centroid/radius, or zoom to expand
   - Click on boundary polygon → `map-click-region`
   - Click on empty → `map-click-empty`

3. **Event wiring**: Restore `county-options-loaded` and `ecoregion-options-loaded` events. Wire `loadBoundaries()` to actually fetch and add boundary data.
