`int_species_universe.sql` that counts `inat_obs_data.observations` per
canonical_name (parallel to `checklist_count_agg`, reading the source

carry the new count.

without altering the existing `specimen_count` or `occurrence_count`
semantics (per RESEARCH Open Question 2, locked in STATE.md).

species.json includes `inat_obs_count` for every species; the OCC-02/03
