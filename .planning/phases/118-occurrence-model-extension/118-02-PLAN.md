from `inat_obs_data.observations`, add the `source` discriminator literal to
ALL three arms, NULL-fill the four new iNat-specific columns (`image_url`,

never broken at HEAD.

WABA samples, expert iNat observations) into a single mart with a source
discriminator and iNat-specific extension columns. Per STATE.md unified-model
decision, iNat obs reach the frontend via occurrences.parquet (no separate
inat_obs.parquet).

Output: occurrences.parquet with ~44,533 new rows tagged source='inat_obs'
in addition to the existing Ecdysis + WABA provisional rows; the three RED
tests from Plan 01 turn GREEN after `bash data/dbt/run.sh build`.
