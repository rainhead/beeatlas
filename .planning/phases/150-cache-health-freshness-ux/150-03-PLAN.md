<objective>
Implement the page-side cache-prime engine + freshness formatter that surface CACHE-02 (determinate prime progress) and CACHE-04 (Data-as-of freshness label).

Three concurrent surfaces:
1. **`src/manifest.ts`**: add `parseGeneratedAt()` (Date | null) and `formatFreshness(generatedAt, now?, locale?)` (string | null) per CONTEXT D-09 / D-12 and UI-SPEC §Copywriting; promote `loadManifest` from private to exported so the orchestrator + freshness sub-line consumer (Plan 04 `<bee-atlas>`) can share the module-level promise cache (PATTERNS.md §S4).
2. **`src/prime-orchestrator.ts`** (NEW): own the cold-start byte-progress fetch loop, the `online`-event re-prime listener, the `caches.match()` ready probe, and `localStorage` persistence of total bytes (D-04). Emit two CustomEvents on `window`: `cache-prime-progress` (during streaming) and `cache-state-changed` (after each asset completes / on cold-start probe). Replace the inline `probeAndReprime()` in `src/app-entry.ts` per PATTERNS.md §`src/app-entry.ts`.
3. **`src/app-entry.ts`**: rewire — drop the inline `probeAndReprime` + `online` listener; replace with a side-effect import of `./prime-orchestrator.ts`. The orchestrator subsumes both call sites.

Plus Wave 0 tests in `src/tests/prime-orchestrator.test.ts` and `src/tests/freshness.test.ts` that pin behavior BEFORE the implementation lands.

Purpose: Plan 04's `<bee-atlas>` listens for `cache-prime-progress` + `cache-state-changed` and renders the ready-pill + popover. Without this plan, Plan 04 has no event source.

Output: a working byte-by-byte prime that reports honest progress on cold start, resumes after offline-mid-prime via the `online` listener, and exposes a `formatFreshness` helper that renders the locked UI-SPEC strings.
