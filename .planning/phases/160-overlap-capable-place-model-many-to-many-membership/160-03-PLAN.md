<objective>
Wave 2 (exports): recompute per-place counts (`places.json`) and per-place SVG
maps from the `occurrence_places` bridge instead of the dropped scalar
`place_slug`. An occurrence that belongs to multiple places must count toward —
and appear on the map of — every place it is in (D-05/SC-3).

Purpose: `_query_counts` and `generate_place_maps` currently read `place_slug`
directly off `occurrences.parquet`; that column no longer exists after 160-02.
They must JOIN the bridge. This turns the 160-01 double-count fixtures GREEN.
Output: bridge-driven `places.json` counts and `place-maps/{slug}.svg`.
</objective>
