**Slug-naming convention (planner decision, IMMUTABLE after first publish):**
`slug_for(name)` lowercases the `WLA_Name`, replaces non-`[a-z0-9]` runs with `-`,
strips leading/trailing `-`, and appends `-wildlife-area` if the slugified name does
not already end in `-wildlife-area`. This disambiguates WDFW areas from any
existing/future place with a bare geographic name (e.g. `methow-wildlife-area` not
`methow`, which could collide with a town/valley place). The 33 slugs are the
slugified forms of the 33 public-list `WLA_Name` values; the exact list is realized
when the script runs in Plan 02 and is recorded in that plan's SUMMARY.
