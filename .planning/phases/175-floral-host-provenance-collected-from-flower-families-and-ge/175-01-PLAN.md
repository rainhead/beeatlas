<objective>
Resolve every observed host-plant taxon to its botanical FAMILY and GENUS, aggregate per bee species into
distinct-sample-counted family/genus hosts, and emit a species_hosts.json sidecar — without touching any
enforced dbt mart contract. inaturalist_data.taxon_lineage_extended is Anthophila-only (no plant taxa), so
plant family resolution is the one new piece; everything else mirrors an exact in-repo analog.

Output: inaturalist_data.host_plant_lineage table + host-plant-lineage run.py step; dbt source +
stg_inat__host_plant_lineage view + int_species_host_plants external-parquet intermediate; species_hosts.json
producer in species_export.py.
</objective>
