Add a pipeline step that exports the dbt-built `occurrences` table from the DuckDB sandbox into a standalone `occurrences.db` SQLite file, then wire `nightly.sh` to upload it content-hashed and surface it as the `occurrences_db` key in `manifest.json`.

Output: `data/sqlite_export.py`, a new STEP in `data/run.py`, two new lines in the `nightly.sh` upload section, and pytest coverage of the generator.
