<objective>
Create the upstream dbt rollup that every other Phase 132 plan reads. A new staging view resolves
subfamily/tribe/subgenus names to ancestor `taxon_id`s from `taxa.csv.gz` (D-02), and a new external
parquet mart `higher_taxa` produces one taxon_id-keyed row per higher-rank taxon with rolled-up counts
and membership (D-01, D-10), grouped by ancestor `taxon_id` — never by rank-name strings (the core
PAGE-01 requirement).

Purpose: Replace string-grouping with a hierarchy-keyed rollup so genus/tribe/subgenus pages and the
new subfamily pages become thin presenters of a single dbt-produced artifact (the "dbt is sole
producer" invariant).
Output: `target/sandbox/higher_taxa.parquet` (via dbt build), an enforced schema.yml contract, and a
pytest suite pinning count-equivalence against the RESEARCH baselines.
</objective>
