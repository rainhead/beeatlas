## Deviations from Plan

### Auto-fixed Issues

**1. [Rule 2 - Missing critical functionality] Added DISTINCT ON deduplication before eco_fallback CTE**

- **Found during:** Task 1 — implementing the ecoregion spatial join
- **Issue:** ecoregions table has 66 individual polygon rows for 9 distinct ecoregion names (e.g., "Strait of Georgia/Puget Lowland" has 54 rows). ST_Within against individual polygons can produce multiple matching rows per occurrence/observation when a point sits inside a multi-polygon ecoregion. Without deduplication, the UNION ALL in final_eco would produce duplicates.
- **Fix:** Added `eco_dedup AS (SELECT DISTINCT ON (occurrence_id) ...)` CTE between `with_eco` and `eco_fallback` in both ecdysis and samples export functions. Same dedup applied to county join (via LEFT JOIN which avoids duplicates natively, but eco needed explicit dedup).
- **Files modified:** data/export.py
- **Commit:** 7058f06

**2. [Rule 3 - Blocking issue] Parquet files not force-added (gitignored build artifacts)**
