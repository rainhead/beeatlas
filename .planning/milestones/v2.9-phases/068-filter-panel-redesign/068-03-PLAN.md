**Placement rationale (D-10 deviation):** D-10 states the filter control lives "inside `<bee-map>`". That cannot mean as a DOM child of the `<bee-map>` shadow root, because `<bee-map>` is a pure presenter (CLAUDE.md Architecture Invariants) — it may not own app state or receive components that emit state-changing events upward. "Inside `<bee-map>`" in D-10 means *visually overlaid on top of the map area*, which is achieved by placing `<bee-filter-panel>` inside the `.content` div (which has `position: relative`) alongside `<bee-map>`. The `position: absolute` + `top: 0.5em` + `right: calc(0.5em + 6rem)` styling produces the intended visual result without violating the presenter invariant.
