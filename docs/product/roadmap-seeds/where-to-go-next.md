The second use case in the under-served **"work"** half: help a volunteer decide **where to
collect next**. Three inputs, all public:

- **Gaps** — where bees haven't been collected (falls straight out of existing occurrence data).
- **Access / permission** — where collecting is allowed (already curated in `content/places.toml`).
- **Bloom** — what's flowering where, *when*. **iNaturalist vends phenological data** (flowering /
  budding annotations on plant observations), so this is far less blocked than first assumed.

No auth required. Less personal than "me and my progress," so likely sequenced after it — but the
phenology-ingest piece could be scoped independently.

Part of the **"work" half** (see [two-halves.md](../two-halves.md)).
